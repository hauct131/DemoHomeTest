# How to get the most out of your Digital Signs

Here is a quick list of ideas and things you can achieve with OptiSigns to get the most out of your digital signs:

- Besides images and videos, you can also upload any [PDF, PowerPoint, and many other file types](what-types-of-files-are-supported.md)
- Display a Website. This can be an [ordinary website](how-to-use-the-website-app-and-display-urls-on-optisigns.md), a more [advanced website](how-to-use-advanced-website-app.md), a [scrolling website](how-to-use-scrolling-website-app.md), or a [website/dashboard requiring a login](how-to-use-the-web-scripting-app.md). We can even handle [websites needing 2FA](link).
- [Add QR Codes](how-to-add-a-qr-code-in-designs-using-optisigns-designer.md) to your screens
- [Use our Templates](navigating-through-optisigns-template-library.md) to save time creating content
- [Use Push to Screens](push-contents-to-your-screens.md) feature to easily schedule content changes or temporary take over
- [Use Schedules](creating-and-using-schedules-with-optisigns.md) or [Live & Expire](how-to-set-an-asset-to-live-or-expire-at-specific-time.md) for more scheduling options
- OptiSigns integrate easily with your existing tools: [Google Slides](how-to-use-google-slides-with-optisigns.md), [Calendar](how-to-use-google-calendar-with-optisigns.md), [Canva](how-to-best-use-canva-with-optisigns.md), [PowerBI](how-to-display-microsoft-power-bi-dashboards-reports-with-optisigns.md), and [more](https://www.optisigns.com/apps)
- [Explore our Feeds](https://feeds.optisigns.com/), it's curated, prebuilt fresh content for your screens.
- If you are looking to add more screens, you can [order Pre-Configured Devices](https://app.optisigns.com/app/s/android-devices) from us. They'll ship directly to your locations and save you time setting them up.

Trying to do something else? Search our [knowledge base, we have 400+ articles](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/en-us) covering many use cases, scenarios.

Still haven't found what you're looking for? We really do want to learn more & help you, just email us at [our support team.](mailto:support%40optisigns.com "our support team")
