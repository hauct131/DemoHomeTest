# How to use the TechCrunch News App

### In this article, we'll explain how to use the TechCrunch News app on OptiSigns.

- [What You'll Need](#WhatYouNeed)
- [Creating a TechCrunch News Asset](#Creating)

The OptiSigns TechCrunch app allows you to display TechCrunch News on your digital signs. It covers the most up-to-date Startups, AI, Venture, Security, Crypto, Apps, Fintech, Hardware news and more.

QR Codes are displayed, which a viewer can scan with their phone to read the relevant article. TechCrunch News can also be displayed as [Scrolling Strip.](how-to-create-a-scrolling-strip-bars.md)

Below is an example screenshot of how it look on your screens, you can view a live example [here](https://social-player.optisigns.com/techcrunch/?asset_id=md9JJe4Abuidwem2x):

## Creating a TechCrunch News Asset

First, you will need to have your screens set up and paired. For more information on how to do that, click [here](link).

Then log on to our portal: <http://app.optisigns.com/>

Go to **Files/Assets**, Click on "App" and add **TechCrunch** on your Account.

[Image: 45516237886867]

Click **TechCrunch**:

[Image: 45516237891347]

Enter your TechCrunch Wall App information:

[Image: 45516237896851]

- Name: Name of your TechCrunch Wall app, this is the name of the app in your asset list. It will **not** be displayed on your screens.
- Category: Select the region and category that you want to focus on. You can check as many as you want.
- Theme: Select Light or Dark Theme.
- Speed: Select how fast you want the app to scroll or switch between posts. You can also customize your speed if you select Custom.

[Image: 45516216045331]

- Font Size: Set the font size for the TechCrunch Wall app.

Clicking **Advanced**opens up additional options:

[Image: 45516237900179]

- Date Format: Choose from a set of pre-formatted dates, or select a custom date format:

 [Image: 45516216052627]

- Scrolling Strip Font Size: If you put the TechCrunch News Wall app to a SplitScreen zone that is a scrolling strip, you can set how big you want the scrolling text to be.

After you've configured the TechCrunch News app to your liking, hit **Save**. Your TechCrunch app will appear as an Asset in OptiSigns, and it can now be directly assigned to a screen or used in a Playlist. This asset can then be edited at any time from the Files/Assets tab.
