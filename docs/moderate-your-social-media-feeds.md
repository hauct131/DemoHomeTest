# Moderate your social media feeds

If you are using OptiSigns to display your social feed like Instagram, Facebook, and Twitter, sometimes you want the option to review/approve certain posts before they show up on your screens. This is particularly useful when you use Hashtags as your social feeds, as this gives you a chance to review the content.

OptiSigns has a built-in content moderation feature that allows you to do just that.

#### **Here's how to use it:**

If you haven't created your [Instagram](how-to-create-and-use-instagram-wall.md), [Facebook](how-to-use-the-facebook-page-app.md), or [Twitter](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/en-us/articles/360016375473) wall, you can go ahead and create one.

In Instagram, Facebook, and Twitter app settings, click Advance, and there's an option for Moderation.

[Image: mceclip0.png]

By default "Always show new posts" is checked, this means that all new posts will automatically show up on your wall/feed.

You can uncheck, and click on the wheel [Image: mceclip1.png] button to moderate the content.

You then can filter, and search for posts.

And click [Image: mceclip3.png] to hide the post or [Image: mceclip5.png] to delete the post (delete means the post will be deleted from the list in OptiSigns only, not from your account)

[Image: mceclip2.png]

, you have learned how to moderate your social media feed on your digital signage screens.

If you have any questions or suggestions, please feel free to contact us at [our support team](mailto:our support team)
