# What hardware and devices are supported?

### You can run OptiSigns on any:

- OptiSigns Devices ([Where to Buy](where-to-buy-optisigns-devices.md))
 - [OptiStick](optistick.md)
 - [Pro Player](optisigns-pro-player-advanced-features.md)
 - [ProMax Player](promax.md)
 - [OptiKiosk series](kiosk-setup-hub.md)
- Android or Amazon devices
- [Windows](windows.md), [Mac](macos.md), [Linux](linux.md) PCs
- [Raspberry Pi](raspberry-pi.md)
- [ChromeOS](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/en-us/articles/360054033374)
- [BrightSign](brightsign-players.md)
- [LG Commercial TVs](lg-webos-signage-commercial-grade.md)
- [Apple TV](how-to-install-optisigns-player-on-apple-tv.md)
- [MacOS](macos.md)
- iOS
- [Samsung TVs](setting-up-your-samsung-smart-signage-platform-sssp-commercial-grade.md)
