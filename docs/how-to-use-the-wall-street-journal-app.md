# How to use the Wall Street Journal App

### In this article, we'll explain how to use The Wall Street Journal app on OptiSigns.

- [What You'll Need](#WhatYouNeed)
- [Creating a The Wall Street Journal Asset](#Creating)

The OptiSigns CBC app allows you to display The Wall Street Journal on your digital signs. It covers the most up-to-date Opinion pieces, World News stories, US Business pieces, Market news, Technology, and Lifestyle pieces.

QR Codes are displayed, which a viewer can scan with their phone to read the relevant article. The Wall Street Journal can also be displayed as [Scrolling Strip.](how-to-create-a-scrolling-strip-bars.md)

[Image: 45517575893779]

## What You'll Need

- An [**OptiSigns-capable device**](what-hardware-and-devices-are-supported.md)
- An OptiSigns [**Free plan or higher**](https://www.optisigns.com/pricing)**\***
- A screen, [**set up and paired**](optisigns-getting-started-guide.md)

**\*** If using a Free plan, make sure you're using a [Free-plan capable device](what-do-i-get-with-an-optisigns-free-plan.md)

## Creating a The Wall Street Journal Asset

First, you will need to have your screens set up and paired. For more information on how to do that, click [here](link).

Then log on to our portal: <http://app.optisigns.com/>

Go to **Files/Assets**, Click on "App" and add **The Wall Street Journal** on your Account.

[Image: 45517575898387]

Click **The Wall Street Journal**:

[Image: 45517616582931]

Enter your Wall Street Journal App information:

[Image: 45517616584211]

- Name: Name of your Wall Street Journal app, this is the name of the app in your asset list. It will **not** be displayed on your screens.
- Category: Select the region and category that you want to focus on. You can check as many as you want.
- Theme: Select Light or Dark Theme.
- Speed: Select how fast you want the app to scroll or switch between posts. You can also customize your speed if you select Custom.

[Image: 45517616589715]

- Font Size: Set the font size for the Wall Street Journal app.

Clicking **Advanced**opens up additional options:

[Image: 45517575903763]

- Date Format: Choose from a set of pre-formatted dates, or select a custom date format:

 [Image: 45517575905171]

- Scrolling Strip Font Size: If you put the Wall Street Journal app to a SplitScreen zone that is a scrolling strip, you can set how big you want the scrolling text to be.

After you've configured the Wall Street Journal app to your liking, hit **Save**. Your Wall Street Journal app will appear as an Asset in OptiSigns, and it can now be directly assigned to a screen or used in a Playlist. This asset can then be edited at any time from the Files/Assets tab.
